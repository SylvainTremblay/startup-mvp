MVP

- Simple and first thing you can give to first set of users
- Talk to users before building MVP
- Launch something quickly (bad or not): weeks not month
- Get initials customers
- Get feedback from initial customers
- Iterate
- start with limited set of functionalities
- timebox your spec: 3-4 weeks max
- write your spec
- Cut your spec

Characteristics
- Serve at least one specific targeted audience
- address one key point or pain point
- has a distinct well design user experience, functionnal, usable
- can be built and launch quickly
